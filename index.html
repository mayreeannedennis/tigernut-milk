Tigernut milk is a healthy drink that is highly appreciated for its healthy and nutritive value It has high fiber content, protein, sugars, healthy fat as well as minerals such as magnesium, potassium and vitamins such as vitamin C&E. It insoluble dietary fibre content is higher compared to other natural source of fibre such as apples Insoluble fibre helps diabetic patient remain healthy, help people on ketogenic journey as it keep you fuller and promote healthy bowel movement.

It is also an alternative to cow milk especially for those who are lactose intolerant Vitamins which it contains helps to lower cholesterol levels, boost fertility in both men and women and minerals like magnesium and potassium which is present in tigernut milk helps in lowering blood pressure and maintaining heartbeat
